# Design Context - Casa Vegueta

## Target Audience
International tourists looking for unique vacation rental in historic Las Palmas de Gran Canaria.

## Use Cases
- Discover the property and its features
- Understand the historic neighborhood and things to do nearby
- Book directly or via Airbnb/Booking

## Brand Personality / Tone
Modern, fresh, warm. Mediterranean editorial feel — like a luxury travel magazine feature.
Historic charm meets contemporary sophistication.

## Aesthetic Direction: "Mediterranean Journal"
An editorial, magazine-inspired design. Warm terracotta and sand tones, elegant serif headings,
generous whitespace, asymmetric layouts. The site should feel like stepping into a curated
travel experience, not a generic rental listing.

## Palette
- Terracotta (#C26A4B) - primary accent
- Olive (#5E7352) - secondary accent / nature
- Sand (#F0E6D8) - warm backgrounds
- Cream (#FDFAF5) - page background
- Warm charcoal (#2C2825) - text
- Navy (#1E3148) - deep accent

## Typography
- Headings: Instrument Serif (editorial, elegant)
- Body: Sora (clean, modern, geometric)
